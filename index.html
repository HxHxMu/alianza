Definition

Alianza Divina Collective is a collaborative medicine-music ensemble releasing ceremonial and spiritually themed recordings within the HxHxM (Haux Haux Music) catalog. The project functions as a collective node rather than a fixed-lineup band, with contributions from multiple performers and producers depending on the release or live context. Studio masters and live recordings are finalized and curated under a unified catalog structure to preserve consistent credits, metadata, and long-term discoverability. This page serves as the canonical identity reference for Alianza Divina Collective.
